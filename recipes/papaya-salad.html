Chicken Papaya Salad

    Main ingredients

  • 1 barbecue chicken, skin and bones removed, meat shredded
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 3/4 cup fresh coriander sprigs
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 2 long fresh red chillies, thinly sliced
  • 600g piece papaya, deseeded, peeled, cut into wedges
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts, salted, chopped

    • Dressing ingredients

  • 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp fish sauce

    Steps to cook

  1. Step 1

    For the dressing, stir the ingredients in a small bowl until the sugar dissolves. Season with a pinch of salt. Set aside.

  2. Step 2

    Combine the chicken, onion, coriander, mint and chilli in a bowl. Add three-quarters of the dressing and toss to combine.

  3. Step 3

    Arrange chicken mixture and papaya on a platter. Drizzle with the remaining dressing and sprinkle with peanuts.
